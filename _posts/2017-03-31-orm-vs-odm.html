ORM:

           Leverages existing knowledge and skills related to OOP.
           It also uses associations rules for mapping of data
-one to one
-one to many 
-many to one
-many to many 
          This is what ORM actually does which would require lot of time hence wee have moved to ODM concepts...
                    

ODM:

           Mongodb is the example of the document database-you would use an ODM to transfer between the objects in code and document representation of the data.
